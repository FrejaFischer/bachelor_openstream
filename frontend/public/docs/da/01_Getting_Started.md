# 1. Getting Started

Welcome to the OpenStream Admin Site documentation. This guide will walk you through the essential features and functionalities of the platform.

## 1.1. Introduction

This documentation is designed to help you get the most out of the OpenStream Admin Site. Whether you are a new user or an experienced administrator, you will find valuable information here.

## 1.2. Logging In

To access the admin site, you need to log in with your credentials. The login screen is the first thing you will see.

- **Username**: Your registered email address.
- **Password**: Your secret password.

If you have trouble logging in, please contact support.

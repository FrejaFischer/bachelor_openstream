## 4.1. Managing Displays

Here you can see a list of all your registered displays. You can see their status (online/offline) and what content they are currently showing.

## 4.2. Opening a Screen

The "Open Screen" feature allows you to preview a slideshow on a virtual display directly in your browser. This is useful for testing content before deploying it to a physical screen.

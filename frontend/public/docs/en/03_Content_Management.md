# 3. Content Management

This section covers everything related to creating, editing, and managing your content.

## 3.1. Managing Content

The "Manage Content" page is where you can see all your slideshows. You can create new slideshows, edit existing ones, or delete them.

## 3.2. Editing a Slideshow

The Content Engine is a powerful tool for building your slideshows.

### 3.2.1. Adding Slides

You can add different types of slides:
- Image slides
- Video slides
- Web content slides

### 3.2.2. Slide Settings

Each slide has its own settings, such as duration and transition effects.

## 3.3. Slideshow Playlists

Playlists allow you to group multiple slideshows and schedule them to play at different times.
